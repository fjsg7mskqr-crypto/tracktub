# Outreach templates (compliant)

Replace `[Name]`, `[City]`, `[N]`. Send from a real domain email (e.g. `hello@tracktub.com`), not no-reply bulk.

## LinkedIn connection note (300 chars max)

```
Hi [Name]—I saw you manage STR in [City]. I'm researching how operators 
document hot tubs between guest turnovers (not selling yet). Would you be open 
to a 20-min call? Happy to share what other PMs are doing.
```

## Cold email (business contact only)

**Subject:** Quick research—hot tub turnovers at STR properties?

```
Hi [Name],

I'm building tools for STR operators who manage multiple properties with hot tubs. 
Before writing code, I'm interviewing PMs about how they prove tubs are guest-ready 
before check-in—and what breaks when you're past ~5 properties.

Would you have 25 minutes this or next week? I'll send a one-page summary of 
what peers are doing; no pitch unless you want a pilot.

If not a fit, a reply "not me" helps too.

Thanks,
[Your name]
[Calendly link]
```

## Facebook group (public post)

```
Hosts with hot tubs—how do you document tub condition between guests? 
(Camera roll, checklist, PMS note, nothing?)

Doing research for operators with 3+ listings. Offering a free 20-min call to 
swap notes—DM me if you manage multiple tubs in [region]. Not a product pitch 
on the first call.
```

## Follow-up (5 days, no reply)

**Subject:** Re: hot tub turnover research

```
Hi [Name]—bumping once in case this got buried. If you're not the right person, 
who handles turnovers at your company? Either way, appreciate it.
```

## After interview (pilot invite)

```
Thanks again. Based on what you shared, a 2-week concierge pilot might help: 
your staff send photos, we return a weekly per-property summary ($49–99 pilot). 
Interested in being one of 3 operators we test with?
```

## What never to send

- Pretending to be a guest on Airbnb
- “I saw your listing…” + pitch in Airbnb inbox
- Mass CC to scraped emails
- False urgency or fake reviews

# Concierge pilot playbook (2–3 operators, 2–4 weeks)

Run **before** relying on the app for AI or automation. Goal: paid renewal or referral.

## Offer

- **Price:** $49–149/mo for up to 10 properties (adjust by market)
- **Delivery:** Staff send photos via shared album or Google Form → you categorize and send weekly PDF/summary per property
- **SLA:** Weekly summary by Monday; same-day flag if subject line includes `URGENT`

## Onboarding checklist

- [ ] Signed simple pilot agreement (email OK)
- [ ] List of properties: name, address, tub notes, assigned staff contact
- [ ] Defined photo SOP (minimum 4 shots: wide, waterline, panel, cover/filter)
- [ ] Shared intake channel (album link or form URL in `concierge-intake-template.md`)
- [ ] Billing collected (Stripe payment link or invoice)

## Weekly deliverable template

Per property:

1. Last turnover date and who submitted
2. Photo thumbnails (or links) with your tags
3. Open issues (`water_cloudy`, `cover_damage`, etc.)
4. Recommended actions before next check-in
5. Recurring maintenance due (from your spreadsheet)

## Exit criteria

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Renews month 2 or refers peer | Migrate to TrackTub MVP; discount first month |
| Uses summaries in owner/guest dispute | Capture quote for landing page |
| Stops sending photos week 2 | Interview why; do not build features they ignored |
| Zero renewal after 3 pilots | Revisit beachhead (service companies) |

## Migration to app

When MVP is ready: create org, import properties CSV, invite staff to PWA turnover flow, compare time-to-submit vs. concierge week.

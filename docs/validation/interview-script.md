# STR operator interview script (~30 min)

Use with operators who manage **3+ STR properties with hot tubs**. Record notes in `interview-log.csv` (same folder).

## Intro (2 min)

> Thanks for taking the time. I'm researching how STR operators document hot tub condition between guest turnovers—not selling yet. Your honest workflow matters more than praise.

## Context (5 min)

1. How many STR properties do you actively manage? How many have hot tubs?
2. Who physically checks the tub between guests—you, staff, or a vendor?
3. How often do guests check in with a hot tub amenity? (weekly / daily peak?)

## Current workflow (10 min)

4. Walk me through the **last turnover** where the tub mattered. What did you do step by step?
5. Where do photos or notes live today? (Camera roll, iCloud, WhatsApp, PMS, nothing?)
6. Have you ever had a **guest complaint, refund, or owner dispute** about the tub? What evidence did you wish you had?
7. What reminders or maintenance do you track today? (Spreadsheet, calendar, head only?)
8. What breaks when you're scaling past ~5 properties?

## Pain and value (8 min)

9. On a scale of 1–10, how painful is tub documentation vs. other turnover tasks?
10. What would "good enough" proof look like before check-in? (Photo set? Chemistry log? Signature?)
10b. **(Synthesis bridge)** After a busy weekend of guests, does the tub water ever go cloudy or lose sanitizer before the next check-in? What do you do about it today? *(Tag `chem_pain` if yes.)*
11. If this saved one bad review or $200 refund per quarter, what would you pay per property per month?
12. Would you try a **paid pilot this month** if we handled summaries manually for 2 weeks?

## Close (5 min)

13. Can I follow up with a one-page sample weekly report for your properties?
14. Who else in your market manages multiple tubs? (Referral)

## Success signals (tag in log)

- `workaround` — Already uses brittle system (albums, chat, checklist)
- `pilot_yes` — Would try paid pilot this month
- `pain_high` — Score 8+ on pain question
- `referral` — Offered intro to another operator
- `chem_pain` — Confirms tub water crashes after heavy guest use (validates the chemistry-aware layer; ≥40% promotes §8.8 from gated to committed)

# TrackTub positioning (STR beachhead)

## One-liner

Guest-ready hot tub proof for every turnover—built for short-term rental operators.

## Job to be done

STR operators and property managers need to prove the hot tub was safe and guest-ready before check-in, catch issues between turnovers, and reduce disputes with owners and guests.

## Who we serve (phase 1)

| Role | Pain | Promise |
|------|------|---------|
| STR operator / PM | No single view across properties; photos scattered in camera rolls or chat | One cockpit: last visit, photos, tasks, alerts per property |
| STR staff / cleaners | Unclear SOP at the property; no structured handoff | Guided turnover: photos + checklist in under 10 minutes |

## What we are not

- A generic home maintenance app
- A consumer hot tub hobby app
- AI-only categorization without human confirmation

## Hooks (use in outreach)

1. **Guest-ready compliance** — Show the tub was ready on check-in day.
2. **Dispute insurance** — Timestamped visual record when guests claim cleanliness or safety issues.
3. **Multi-property cockpit** — One screen for every tub you manage.

## Pricing hypothesis (pilot)

- $49–149/mo concierge pilot (up to 10 properties, manual delivery)
- Target product: $8–15/property/month at 5+ properties

## Kill / pivot signals

After 20 interviews and 3 paid pilots: zero renewals and staff never submit turnovers → pivot to service companies or compliance-only reports.
